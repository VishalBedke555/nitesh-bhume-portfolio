About Me

I am a Research Associate at the Institute of Structural Analysis, Leibniz University Hannover, where I develop advanced simulations for underwater sound propagation and structural-acoustic interactions in offshore wind farm settings.

With a Master's degree in Computational Sciences in Engineering from TU Braunschweig and extensive experience in finite element analysis, I bring a strong foundation in computational engineering and a passion for solving complex technical challenges.

My expertise spans multiple FEA software platforms including Abaqus, Ansys Workbench, COMSOL Multiphysics, and more. I have contributed to projects at leading organizations including Audi AG and ebm-papst, focusing on structural analysis, thermal simulations, and optimization.
